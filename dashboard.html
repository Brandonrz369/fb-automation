Safety Rules

Critical rules to prevent account restrictions

NEVER put links in main post

Put links in FIRST COMMENT only. Links in main text kill reach by 80% and trigger spam detection.

NEVER post identical content to multiple groups

Facebook AI fingerprints detect this instantly. Need 5+ text variations per content piece.

Wait 65-140 minutes between posts

Faster posting looks like bot behavior. Randomize delays within this range.

Max 3-5 posts per day

Higher volume triggers rate limits and spam detection.

1 post per group every 14 days

More frequent posting to the same group looks spammy to admins.

Engage 10:1 ratio

For every promotional post, engage with 10 non-business posts (likes, comments on friends' content).

Use raw photos, not graphics

Canva graphics and text overlays look like ads. Authentic photos perform better.

Sound like a neighbor, not a business

Community groups respond better to helpful neighbors than corporate marketing.

Emergency Procedures

What to do when things go wrong

CRITICAL: Account Restricted
  1. STOP all automation immediately
    touch ~/fb-automation/data/paused.lock
  2. Do NOT try to log in repeatedly - makes it worse
  3. Wait 24-48 hours with zero activity
  4. Log in from phone - scroll, like friends' photos
  5. After 72 hours - resume at 50% volume (2 posts/day max)
HIGH: Multiple Posts Deleted
  1. Pause automation for 72 hours
  2. Review which posts were deleted - identify pattern
  3. Disable problem groups in groups.yaml
  4. Manual activity only for 1 week
  5. Resume with ONE post per day
MEDIUM: CAPTCHA Appearing
  1. Complete the CAPTCHA carefully
  2. Pause automation for 24 hours
  3. Review posting frequency - may need to slow down
  4. Resume with caution
